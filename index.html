We cover NSE cash market (intraday & swing), Nifty/BankNifty options (weekly & monthly), and select commodity calls. All signals are for NSE/BSE listed instruments only.

Free channel: 2-3 intraday signals + 1 swing pick per week. Premium: 5-8 intraday signals, 2-3 swing picks, 3-4 options calls, and real-time updates throughout the day.

Absolutely. Every signal comes with a defined entry price, stop-loss, and 2-3 target levels. We never post a signal without risk management — that's our core principle.

Yes! Our signals are structured so anyone can follow them. We also share educational content explaining the reasoning behind each trade. Many of our most successful members started as complete beginners.
